# Federal OSHA Forms Package — Forms 300, 300A, 301 (Spanish)

## What this file is
Spanish-language version of the federal OSHA recordkeeping forms package, including Form 300 (Registro de Lesiones y Enfermedades Ocupacionales) and Form 300A (Resumen de Lesiones y Enfermedades Ocupacionales). Issued by U.S. Department of Labor, Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA). Spanish. OMB No. 1218-0176. Same legal authority as English version (29 CFR Part 1904).

## What it's for / when it's used
Same purpose as the English package; provided for Spanish-speaking employers and employees. Employers may use the Spanish forms for their own recordkeeping if their workforce is primarily Spanish-speaking. The legal requirements and column structure are identical to the English version.

**Key Spanish-language field labels:**
- Form 300 title: "Formulario OSHA-300 — Registro de Lesiones y Enfermedades Ocupacionales"
- Form 300A title: "Formulario OSHA-300A — Resumen de Lesiones y Enfermedades Ocupacionales"
- Column headings: Identifique la Persona (A-C), Describa el Caso (D-F), Clasifique el Caso (G-M)
- Death = "Muerte"; Days away from work = "Días Fuera del Trabajo"; Job transfer/restriction = "Transferencia o Restricción de Trabajo"
- Illness types: Lesión, Desórdenes de la Piel, Condición Respiratoria, Envenenamiento, Pérdida de la Audición, Otra Enfermedad
- 300A uses NAICS code field (not SIC) — more current than the English version header
- Posting instruction: "Fije este Resumen a partir del 1ro. de febrero hasta el 30 de abril del año siguiente"

## Key information an LLM needs
All recording criteria, posting deadlines, retention requirements, and privacy rules are identical to the English version. See `osha-300-forms-package-en` for the full details. This file is the Spanish translation only — no substantive differences.

**Confidentiality notice (top of Form 300, Spanish):** "Atención: Este formulario contiene información relacionada a la salud de los empleados y debe ser usada de manera que proteja la confidencialidad de éstos al máximo posible, mientras la información sea usada para propósitos de seguridad y salud ocupacional."

**Note on California:** California employers are subject to Cal/OSHA (CCR Title 8), not federal OSHA (29 CFR Part 1904). Spanish-speaking California employers should use the Cal/OSHA forms if available in Spanish — check dir.ca.gov/dosh.
