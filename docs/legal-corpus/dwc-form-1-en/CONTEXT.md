## What it's for / when it's used
Filed when an employee is injured at work or contracts a work-related illness. The employer is required by CA Labor Code §5401 to provide this form to the employee within one working day of learning about the injury. The employee fills in Part A (employee's section), keeps one copy, and returns the rest to the employer. The employer fills Part B (employer's section) within one working day and sends copies to their claims administrator.

Triggers the formal workers' comp claim process. Once the employer receives the completed form, the claims administrator must authorize up to $10,000 in medical treatment while the claim is being investigated (the "one working day" rule).

## Key information an LLM needs
**Employee section (Part A):** Name, date of birth, home address, date of injury/illness, time of injury, address where injury occurred, description of injury (body part and how it occurred), treating physician/hospital name and address, signature and date.

**Employer section (Part B):** Date employer was notified, name and address of employer, insurance carrier name and address, employer signature and title, date.

**Benefits listed on the form:**
- Medical care (paid by employer)
- Temporary disability payments (approx. 2/3 of average weekly wage while unable to work)
- Permanent disability payments if permanent impairment results
- Supplemental job displacement benefit (SJDB) voucher for retraining if permanently disabled and not offered regular/modified/alternative work
- Death benefits to dependents

**Deadlines:**
- Employer must give form to employee within 1 working day of learning about injury
- Employee has 30 days from date of injury to file; claims filed after 1 year may be barred
- Employer/claims admin must authorize treatment within 1 working day of receiving form

**Key note:** The form instructs the employee to keep the top copy and return all others to the employer. The employer must retain a copy and send copies to the claims administrator.

**No SSN required on this form.** Employee must provide name, DOB, address — not SSN.

**Language:** Bilingual EN/ES side-by-side on all pages.
